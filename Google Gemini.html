Gabzology Signature Products

1. Hydrating Hair Mist

A lightweight, daily spray designed to refresh and moisturize.

Key Ingredients:

  • Distilled water
  • Aloe vera juice
  • Rose water
  • Castor oil
  • Peppermint essential oil
  • Lavender oil
  • Rosemary oil
  • Tea tree essential oil

Benefits for 4C Hair:

This mist is a humectant-rich powerhouse that draws moisture into the hair, while the castor oil helps seal it in. The essential oils stimulate the scalp and promote a healthy environment for growth. It’s perfect for daily hydration and refreshing protective styles.

2. Herbal-Infused Hair & Scalp Oil

A rich, nourishing oil infused with potent botanicals.

Key Ingredients:

  • Avocado oil
  • Essential rosemary oil
  • Dried hibiscus
  • Essential lavender oil
  • Dried fennel
  • Dried turmeric
  • Rosebuds
  • Dried peppermint
  • Peppermint essential oil
  • Honey

Benefits for 4C Hair:

Avocado oil's penetrating properties deliver vitamins deep into the hair shaft. The infused herbs and essential oils are known for their ability to stimulate circulation, promote growth, soothe the scalp, and reduce inflammation. This oil is ideal for scalp massages and hot oil treatments, providing intense nourishment and strength to both natural and bleached hair.
